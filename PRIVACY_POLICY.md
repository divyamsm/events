# Privacy Policy for StepOut

**Last updated: January 3, 2025**

## Introduction

StepOut ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account**: Name, email, phone number (optional), username, password
- **Profile**: Photo, bio, event preferences, location (optional)
- **Events**: Titles, descriptions, locations, dates, photos, categories
- **Content**: Photo comments, event messages, friend invitations

### Automatically Collected
- **Device**: Model, OS version, identifiers
- **Usage**: Features accessed, events viewed, searches
- **Location**: Approximate location (when permitted)

## How We Use Your Information

- Provide and maintain the service
- Show relevant events based on preferences and location
- Send push notifications about events and friends
- Improve features and fix bugs
- Prevent fraud and abuse

## Information Sharing

**We do NOT sell your personal information.**

We share information with:
- **Other Users**: Public event details, profile info, RSVP status
- **Firebase/Google**: Authentication, database, analytics
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law

## Your Rights

- Access and update your profile
- Delete your account anytime
- Manage permissions (location, contacts, camera, photos)
- Opt-out of notifications
- Request data copy

## Data Security

- Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Encryption at rest (Firebase)
- Secure password authentication
- Limited access controls

## Data Retention

- Active until account deletion
- Deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- Some data retained for legal requirements

## Children's Privacy

StepOut is not for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Third-Party Services

We use Firebase (Google) for authentication, database, and analytics.
Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

## Changes to Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be notified via email or in-app.
